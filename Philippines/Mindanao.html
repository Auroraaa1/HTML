Experience the Rich Diversity of Mindanao

The Land of Promise

Mindanao, the second-largest island group, is a land of incredible natural beauty, rich indigenous cultures, and towering mountains. Home to Mount Apo, the country's highest peak, Mindanao offers adventures ranging from whitewater rafting to exploring unique cultural villages and tropical beaches.

Must-Visit Attractions (Click to Search!)

Mount Apo

The highest mountain in the Philippines, located between Davao City and Davao del Sur, offering challenging treks and unique ecosystems.

Siargao Island

The surfing capital of the Philippines, famous worldwide for its iconic Cloud 9 wave, stunning lagoons, and laid-back island lifestyle.

Samal Island

The Island Garden City of Samal near Davao, known for its pristine beaches, resorts, and the Monfort Bat Sanctuary.

Hinatuan Enchanted River

A deep spring river in Surigao del Sur, famed for its clear, deep blue waters and mysterious origins.
