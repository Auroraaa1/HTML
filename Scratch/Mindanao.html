Experience the Rich Diversity of Mindanao

The Land of Promise

Mindanao, the second-largest island group, is a land of incredible natural beauty, rich indigenous cultures, and towering mountains. Home to Mount Apo, the country's highest peak, Mindanao offers adventures ranging from whitewater rafting to exploring unique cultural villages and tropical beaches.

Must-Visit Attractions

Camiguin

Camiguin is known for White Island, hot springs, and old landmarks. The island is small, so travelers can visit many places in one day. White Island has clear water and an open sandbar. Tourists also visit waterfalls and the sunken cemetery. The island stays quiet, which suits visitors who want peaceful surroundings.

Siargao Island

Siargao is known for surfing, quiet beaches, and natural pools. Cloud 9 is the most visited surf spot. The island also has lagoons, caves, and island hopping tours. The roads are simple to follow and many visitors rent motorbikes. The slow pace of the island attracts tourists who want a relaxed trip with outdoor activities.

Davao City

Davao City is known for Mount Apo, fruit plantations, and orderly public areas. Visitors explore the city for its clean markets, high security, and friendly atmosphere. The city is a main center for business and travel in Mindanao. Tourists often visit the Philippine Eagle Center and local museums.
