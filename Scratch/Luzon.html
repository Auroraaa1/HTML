Explore the Wonders of Luzon

The Northern Jewel

Luzon, the largest island in the Philippines, is home to the nation's capital, Manila, and a vast array of historical sites, majestic mountains, pogi ni dwayne and vibrant cultural landscapes. From the cool mountain city of Baguio to the ancient rice terraces of the Cordilleras, Luzon offers a diverse and enriching travel experience.

Must-Visit Attractions

Banaue Rice Terraces

Often called the "Eighth Wonder of the World," these 2,000-year-old terraces were carved into the mountains by ancestors of the indigenous Ifugao people. A UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Vigan City

A beautifully preserved Spanish colonial town in Ilocos Sur. Stroll down Calle Crisologo with its cobblestone streets and ancestral houses to feel transported back in time.

Taal Volcano

Located in Batangas, this is one of the world's smallest active volcanoes, dramatically situated within a lake. It offers incredible views from Tagaytay ridge.
