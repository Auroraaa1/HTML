Discover the Central Islands of Visayas

Islands of Sun and Sea

The Visayas region is the heart of the Philippines, a cluster of beautiful islands known globally for their pristine white-sand beaches, vibrant marine ecosystems, historical significance, and festive culture. This is the ultimate destination for island hopping and diving enthusiasts.

Must-Visit Attractions

Boracay Island

Boracay is known for White Beach. The sand is fine and the water stays clear most months. The island has many hotels, so visitors have many options. Tourists walk along the beachfront, try water activities, and enjoy the evening atmosphere. The island remains one of the most visited places in the country because the beach is easy to access and the area offers many activities.

Chocolate Hills, Bohol

The Chocolate Hills in Bohol are known for their round shapes and large number of hills. Visitors go to the viewing decks for photos and short walks. The hills turn brown during the dry season. The area stays organized for tourists because the viewing spots are simple and easy to reach. Many travelers visit nearby wildlife and river attractions on the same day.

Kawasan Falls

Kawasan Falls in Cebu is known for its blue water and fresh surroundings. Tourists visit for swimming and canyoneering. The walk to the falls is short and follows a clear path. Local guides help visitors move through the river and small jumps. The falls remain popular because the water stays clean and the area is easy to enjoy for day trips.
