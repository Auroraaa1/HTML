Your Adventure Awaits in the Philippines

Explore an archipelago of over 7,000 islands, offering a breathtaking mix of pristine beaches, lush mountains, and vibrant cultural heritage. Discover your next great escape!
Check out our essential travel tips before you go!

Palawan: The Last Frontier

Palawan, consistently ranked as one of the world's most beautiful islands, is known as 'The Last Frontier.' Explore the magnificent limestone karst formations of El Nido, kayak through the clear waters of Coron's sunken WWII shipwrecks, and witness the underground splendor of the Puerto Princesa Subterranean River. It's a sanctuary for rare wildlife and marine biodiversity.

Boracay: Powder White Sand

Reopened and beautifully revitalized, Boracay boasts the world-renowned White Beach, famous for its powder-fine sand and spectacular sunset views. Beyond relaxing, enjoy thrilling watersports like kiteboarding, parasailing, and sunset paraw sailing, making it the perfect blend of natural beauty and vibrant island life.

View Activities

Bohol: Marvels of Nature

Bohol is a haven of natural wonders. Marvel at the geological perfection of the Chocolate Hills, a landscape of over 1,776 conical mounds. Meet the endemic and incredibly small Philippine Tarsier, one of the world's smallest primates, and explore Panglao Island's pristine beaches and world-class dive spots.
